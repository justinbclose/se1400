Competition

What is our competition doing right?

Our competition offers a wide variety of designs and has a strong online presence with active social media engagement.

What is our competition doing wrong?

They often overlook the importance of eco-friendly materials and do not provide options that are safe for pets.

How can we do it better?

We can focus on creating eco-friendly, pet-safe wrapping paper with unique designs that cater to pet lovers. Additionally, we can engage with our audience through interactive content and personalized customer service.

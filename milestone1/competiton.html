Competition

What is our competition doing right?

The competition offers good designs, but there are only a few companies, and their websites look outdated, as if they are no longer in business.

What is our competition doing wrong?

The competition isn't offering a variety of sizes and products; their selection is very limited, and their marketing efforts seem nonexistent.

How can we do it better?

We can offer a variety of products that are eco-friendly and affordable. We believe that focusing on marketing will be the key to our success.

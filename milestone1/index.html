Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will feature pet-themed wrapping paper that is edible and safe for pets. The page will include product descriptions, customer reviews, and blog recommendations with creative ideas.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Most of our media will originate from our design team. We also plan to include a significant portion of photos from consumers who are using and satisfied with the product.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users on this site who love pets and gift-giving will find unique products that offer a great way to reflect their passion for this combination.

Why should a site be built for this?

A dedicated site provides an interface where users can explore a showcase of our products and see them in use.

What would you like users to do at your site?

We want users to browse our products, leave reviews, read others' reviews, and make purchases.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to create a one-stop shop where people can learn about, view, and purchase our products.
